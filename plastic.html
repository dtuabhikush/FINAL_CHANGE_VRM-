Plastic Scrap

We deal in all sort of plastic scrap and polymer waste product like LDPE,HDPE,PS, Plastic raw material, PETE,BOPP,PVC. There are two type of plastic available in market : Thermosetting & Thermoplastic. Thermosetting plastic is not recycable but Thermoplastic is recycable plastic. Recycling of plastic material is very easy because of its chemical and physical properties.

Termosetting Plastic

Termosetting plastic is irriversible in nature.By heating the plastic it turns as hard marerials. Some example of it : Epoxy Resin, Phenolic(Bakelight), Vinyle Ester Resin, Cyanate, Poly Ester.
