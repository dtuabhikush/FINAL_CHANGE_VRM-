Ferrous Metal

We deal in all sort of Ferrous Metals. Ferrous metal composed of iran and has magnetic property. It is economically more superior to recycle steel than to mine iron ore and manipulate it to form new steel. During the recycling process steel does not loose any of its inherent physical properties. Recycling scrap metal desperately reduces energy consumption and material needs compared with refinement from iron ore.

Heavy Metal

Heavy melting Scrap (HMS) is chiefly recyclable steel and wrought iron. There are two major categories: HMS 1 & HMS 2 The basic difference between these two grades are the HMS 1 does not hold galvanized and blackened steel, whereas HMS 2 does. Both of these grades are made up of only obsolete scrap which is scrap that is taken/recovered from disassembled or demolished element.

Light Metal

Light meting scrap (LMS) is mainly recycable steel and wrought iron It include Aluminium scrap, Brass scrap, Zink scrap & light iron scrap
